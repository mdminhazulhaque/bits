Samsung ACE NXT (SM-G313HZ) Bloatware List

30 Jan 2015 android

I have been using Samsung Galaxy ACE NXT (which is also known as Samsung Galaxy V in some regions) for the last 3 months. It is an entry level smartphone with 3G, Dual Sim, and Android 4.4.

The worst thing about Samsung is the bloatware they install for users, I believe. Applications like ChatON, CloudSync, Talkback are useless to me. Plus I am a minimal user who prefers Android with only Google Play Service, nothing else. So I recently rooted my phone and uninstalled all the bloatwares that seem useless to me. It left my system an extra 250MB space. My system is completely functional till now. So it should work for you too. But please be warned.
